Welcome to ANWA

Welcome to ANWA by OMNIYAT. Where there is nothing between you and the sea. This development offers a unique living space in one of the most stunning and unspoiled locations in Dubai. Located in Dubai Maritime City, it offers you access to restaurants, beach clubs, tourist attractions and world-renowned landmarks in less than a 15 minute drive.

ANWA by OMNIYAT not only delivers a superb level of design and specification, but also offers spectacular views, and first class amenities suited to your needs. This homeowner manual aims to provide you with all the information you’ll require, ensuring that living in ANWA by OMNIYAT is as enjoyable as possible.

If you’d like further information or have any queries please don’t hesitate to contact your Omniyat representative.
